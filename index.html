About

Built on Community

Brick City Roleplay is a serious roleplay community built by players, for players. We pride ourselves on immersive storytelling, realistic economy, and a zero-tolerance policy for failRP. Every character has a story to tell — make yours unforgettable.

What We Offer

Server Features

Everything you need for an immersive serious RP experience.

Economy

Fully custom economy system with jobs, businesses, and player-driven markets.

Police

Structured LEO factions with SOPs, ranks, and realistic patrol operations.

Gangs

Territory-based gang system with custom scripts for turf wars and alliances.

Justice

Full DOJ system with court cases, lawyers, and active judges for fair RP.

Housing

Custom housing script with interiors, garages, and furnishing options.

Businesses

Player-owned businesses from restaurants to nightclubs with full management.

Phone

In-game phone with apps, contacts, messaging, and social media mechanics.

Events

Weekly staff-run events with rewards, story arcs, and community involvement.
